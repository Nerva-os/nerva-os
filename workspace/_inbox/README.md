# The Inbox

Drop documents in here to teach your Nerva OS assistant about your business.

## What to drop here
- Brand guidelines, tone of voice docs
- Past sent emails (for voice profiling)
- Pricing sheets, service descriptions
- Client lists, supplier lists
- Any reference doc you want me to learn from

## What I do with it
I read whatever you drop here, ask you what to keep, then file the useful bits into memory so I remember them in every future conversation. You stay in control of what I save.

## How to use it
Drop the file, then say "process the inbox" or "what's in the inbox?".
