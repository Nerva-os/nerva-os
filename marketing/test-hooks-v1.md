# Nerva OS — 5 Test Hooks v1
_2026-06-19. Scroll-stoppers for ad testing. Film quick phone versions, run as ads, scale the winner into the full VSL._

Each hook = the first 5-15 seconds. Relatable, "build-it-with-you" voice. No industry named.

## Hook 1 — "Another me" (clone / team)
If you've ever sat there thinking *"I just need another me"* — someone to handle the admin, the follow-ups, the chasing — stop scrolling. Because I built exactly that, and it gave me my week back.

## Hook 2 — Founder story (most relatable)
Not long ago I was up till midnight building systems for my business by hand — only to redo them the second something changed. Now I just talk to my phone and it's done. Here's what changed, and why I'm never going back.

## Hook 3 — Cost pain
You're probably paying for five different apps right now that don't even talk to each other. What if ONE system did the lot — and you paid once, instead of every single month, forever?

## Hook 4 — The wow ("talk to your business")
What if you could just... talk to your business? Tell it what you need on the drive home — and by the time you're back, it's done. No computer. No apps to learn. You just talk. Let me show you.

## Hook 5 — Anti-guru (trust)
Everyone's out here selling you "AI for your business." Almost nobody shows you how to actually use it. I'm a tradie, not a tech guru — and I'll show you exactly how I built mine, step by step.
